    about the webmaster

    my name is kyu! (she/her)

    well, actually, kyu is the name of my digital identity. my actual name is ada (nickname, at least).

    i'm actually just a design student in my 20s with a passion for art, music and creation of all kinds. i became enamoured with website design after exploring nekoweb and neocities.

    my only experience with html/css before this is through one compulsory subject in my design course where they provided us a template to go off of.

    i do however, have a super smart boyfriend who is like coding jesus... and that's when i got thinking, if i get stuck on anything while making the website, i'll just ask him!

    my code is not perfect, and i'm sure there's better workarounds for a lot of my decisions. despite that, i don't mind if you check and use my code as inspiration for your own website.

    some of my interests

    i love art, playing piano and i like to sew and crochet items.
    in terms of media, i enjoy playing video games, i have an autistic obsession with south park and i love watching movies and reading both books and comics ;D.

    if you would like to learn more about me and the media i consume, click here!
